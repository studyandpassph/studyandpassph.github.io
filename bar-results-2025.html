Bar Exam Results 2025

Official result highlights, coverage, passing grade, and reference links for the 2025 Philippine Bar Examination.

Year: 2025 Category: Bar Exam Results Updated: —

Summary

The Supreme Court (SC) released the official results of the 2025 Bar Examination on January 7, 2026 (Friday afternoon), around four (4) months after the examinations. A total of 5,594 out of 11,420 examinees passed, for an overall passing rate of 48.98%.

Quick Facts

  • Release date: January 7, 2026
  • Passers: 5,594
  • Total examinees: 11,420
  • Passing rate: 48.98%
  • Exam dates: September 7, 10 & 14, 2025
  • Oath-taking: February 6, 2026 (Friday)

On announcement day, the SC displayed the List of Successful Examinees on LED walls at the Supreme Court courtyard, Padre Faura Street, Manila, and opened the gates to the public from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM.

Coverage of Examinations

Aspiring lawyers were tested in the following core subjects (with relative weight):

  • Political and Public International Law (15%)
  • Commercial and Taxation Laws (20%)
  • Civil Law (20%)
  • Labor Law and Social Legislation (10%)
  • Criminal Law (10%)
  • Remedial Law, Legal and Judicial Ethics with Practical Exercises (25%)

Passing Grade

A Bar examinee is generally deemed to have passed if he/she obtains a general average of at least 75% as total weighted score. This score is computed using each subject’s relative weight and the examinee’s raw score as a percentage of the maximum possible score.

The Supreme Court (En Banc) has discretion to make adjustments to the passing percentage, as it did in some previous Bar Examinations (e.g., adjustments from 75% to 74% in certain years; and lower thresholds in earlier years).
