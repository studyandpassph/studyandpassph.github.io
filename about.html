About Study and Pass PH

A study resource hub for exams and templates in the Philippines.

Why Study and Pass PH

Study and Pass PH is built to make exam prep and document templates easier to access. We publish organized result pages, practical reviewers, and ready-to-download templates — all in one place.

For results pages, we prioritize linking official announcements and official PDF lists whenever available. For reviewers and templates, we focus on original, beginner-friendly explanations and downloadable files.

What we publish

  • • Exam results hubs (Bar / Board / CSE)
  • • Reviewers and practice sets
  • • Templates for school, job, and legal needs
  • • Blog guides and study tips

Accuracy + sources

We summarize and organize information for convenience. Always verify results and announcements from official sources.
