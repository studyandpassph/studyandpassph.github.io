Application & Admission Requirements (Overview)

Requirements may change depending on the Bar Bulletin and Supreme Court announcements. Below is a general overview. Always refer to the official Bar Bulletin for the complete and updated list.

  • Completion of the required law degree/units and eligibility to take the Bar (per official rules)
  • Submission of required documents through the official application system (as announced)
  • Payment of application fees (if applicable) and compliance with deadlines
  • Valid identification and required forms on exam days (as specified)

For official requirements and deadlines: place your verified links in the Bar Exam Results page or your Bar Bulletin post.
